Selected Work

LLM Prompt Design · Team of 3 · CHI Award Recipient

LinguaGemma

Advanced Projects in Interactive Media — Sept 2023 – May 2024
View repo ↗

An LLM-based chatbot with two distinct prompt-driven teaching logics, benchmarked against each other in a controlled study.

A chatbot's "personality" is really just its prompt-level rules, and different rule sets should produce measurably different outcomes — so we built two prompt logics and compared them directly.

Designed and hosted the chatbot locally, developing separate prompt logic for a growth-mindset agent and a fixed-mindset control. Assessed impact using word-search recall tests and thematic and semantic analysis of chat logs. The growth-mindset logic demonstrated higher comfort, confidence, and engagement, winning the 2024 CHI undergraduate research competition. Collaborated with two classmates, coordinating study design and project timeline.
